Om Multireligionvalsystemet

Multireligionvalsystemet är utformat för att ge olika trossamfund likvärdig representation genom demokratiska val. Nedan ser du en översikt över aktuella, kommande och avslutade val.

Aktuellt val

Nordic Interfaith Council Election 2025

Democratic election for representatives of the Nordic Interfaith Council. This election follows the principles of the Multi-Religion Election System, ensuring equal representation for all faith traditions across the Nordic countries.

Status: Aktiv

Typ: Council-val

Valperiod: 14 januari 2025 – 15 februari 2025

Inkluderade religioner: Christianity, Islam, Judaism, Buddhism, Hinduism, Sikhism

Kommande val

European Religious Peace Assembly

Election for delegates to the European Religious Peace Assembly, working towards interfaith harmony and conflict resolution through democratic processes.

Status: Kommande

Typ: Representantval

Planerad valperiod: mars 2025

Inkluderade religioner: Christianity, Islam, Judaism, Buddhism

Avslutade val

Global Interfaith Leadership Summit

Completed election for leadership positions in the Global Interfaith Leadership Summit. Results demonstrate the power of democratic religious governance.

Status: Avslutat

Typ: Ledarskapsval

Valperiod: oktober 2024

Totala röster: 1 247

Inkluderade religioner: Christianity, Islam, Judaism, Buddhism, Hinduism
